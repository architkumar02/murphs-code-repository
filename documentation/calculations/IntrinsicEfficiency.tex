The intrisinis efficiency is defined as \eqref{eqn:intEffDef} \cite{knoll_radiation_2009}.
\begin{align}
  \epsilon_{int} = \frac{N_c}{N_i}
\end{align}
where:
\begin{itemize}
  \item[] \nomenclature{$\epsilon_{int}$}{intrisinic efficinecy},
  \item[] \nomenclature{$N_c$}{number of counts recorded by the detector}, and
  \item[] \nomenclature{$N_i$}{quanta of radiation incident upon the detector}.
\end{itemize}
